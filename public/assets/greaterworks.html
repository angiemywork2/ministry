My Beliefs

I believe in the GOD of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. YAHWEH is His Name. I believe in God's Holy Spirit, and that Jesus, Yahshua the Christ is the son of GOD. I believe in the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ. I believe in the gospel of Jesus Christ, for it is the power of GOD unto salvation to everyone that believes. Romans 1:16. I believe that it is by grace that we are saved through faith in Jesus Christ, and not by works. Ephesians 2:8-9. I believe in the remission of sins through the shed blood of Jesus Christ. I believe in the power and demonstration of the Word of GOD through miracles, signs, and wonders.
